SWIFT network (Society for Worldwide Interbank Financial Telecommunication)

The SWIFT network (Society for Worldwide Interbank Financial Telecommunication) is a global messaging platform used by financial institutions to securely exchange information and instructions related to financial transactions. It plays a crucial role in facilitating international banking and finance.

Secure Messaging: SWIFT provides a secure and standardized method for banks and financial institutions to send and receive messages, such as payment orders, confirmations, and other financial communications.

Standardized Formats: SWIFT messages, particularly those in the MT (Message Type) format, ensure that communications are consistent and easily interpretable across different institutions.
